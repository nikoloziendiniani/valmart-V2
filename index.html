FAQ
What are the most popular clothing items?
Some of the most popular styles of clothing are: Jeans: A versatile and timeless wardrobe staple, jeans come in various styles, cuts, and washes. They can be dressed up or down for different occasions. T-shirts: Available in various colors, prints, and designs, T-shirts are a popular choice for everyday wear. They can be worn with jeans, shorts, or skirts and can be layered under jackets, blazers, or cardigans. Hoodies and sweatshirts: Both comfortable and stylish, hoodies and sweatshirts have become essential casual wear items that can be worn year-round. Leggings and yoga pants: Popular for their comfort and versatility, leggings and yoga pants have become a go-to choice for athleisure and casual wear. Little black dress (LBD): A classic and timeless piece, the little black dress is a must-have in every woman's wardrobe. It can be dressed up or down and is suitable for various occasions. Button-up shirts: A staple in both men's and women's wardrobes, button-up shirts can be worn in professional, casual, or formal settings. Sneakers: Stylish and comfortable, sneakers have become a popular footwear choice for casual and athleisure wear. Jackets and coats: Outerwear items like denim jackets, leather jackets, blazers, and trench coats are popular choices for layering and completing an outfit. With these basics of a wardrobe, you can mix-and-match and have multiple outfits to wear.
What is Walmart's policy for returning clothing?
Just like nearly everything we sell, you can return clothing you've purchased from Walmart as long as the return meets certain criteria. We strive to make returns and exchanges easy for you. Start a return on your Walmart app, at Walmart.com, or in-store if: You purchased the clothing item within the past 90 days. The item was not sold by a dealer or reseller on Walmart.com. To return clothing at a local Walmart store in person, bring the clothing in with your receipt to the Customer Service desk. If you don't have a receipt, we can look up purchases you made with a credit card, debit card, with your Walmart app, or online. It may be possible to get a cash refund without a receipt for purchases under $10 or a Walmart gift card for purchases above $10. You'll need to show a valid government-issued ID if you don't have a reciept. You can also return clothing from home if you are a W+ member, schedule an InHome pickup, or schedule a FedEx pickup. No repackaging is required for these options. A final option is to return your item by mail by repackaging it and taking it to a FedEx office or post office. Start your return by going to Walmart.com or using the Walmart app.
What styles of clothing are popular this season?
These types of clothing are always in style, no matter the season. Casual: Comfortable, everyday clothing like jeans, T-shirts, hoodies and sneakers Business casual: A mix of professional and casual attire, often including dress pants or skirts, button-down shirts and blouses Formal: Elegant clothing for special occasions, such as suits, evening gowns and tuxedos Streetwear: Urban-inspired clothing with a focus on comfort and individuality, often featuring bold prints, oversized fits and unique accessories Athleisure: Athletic-inspired clothing that is both stylish and comfortable, such as leggings, sports bras and sneakers. With several types of clothing available, you're sure to find something to wear that meets your needs.
